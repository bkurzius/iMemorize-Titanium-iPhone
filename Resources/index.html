    Instructions

    iMemorize ws designed to help memorize quotations. Simply choose a quote and then either tap on a word t hide it -- and tap on it again to show it. You can also choose "Hide Words" to hide a few words at a time.

    Additional features include:

    • You can enter your own quote in any language
    • Send a quote to a friend -- which includes a link where they can memorize the quote without installing the app
    • Change the font size of the quote so you can read it more easily
