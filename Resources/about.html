About

iMemorize was designed to be a fun and easy way to memorize quotations. It is also available in online and desktop versions at this address: http://www.imemorize.org

If you have any questions or would like to provide feedback regarding this app, please email: bkurzius@gmail.com

Quotations from the Writings of the Bahá’í Faith are copyrighted by the Bahá’í International Community and can be found online at The Bahá’í Reference Library (http://reference.bahai.org)

Quotations from the Christian Bible and Torah are from the King James Version

Quotations from the Qur'an are from "The Holy Koran [Qur'an]" translated by Muhammad Habib Shakir.
