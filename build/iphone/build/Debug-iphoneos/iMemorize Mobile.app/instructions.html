Instructions

To memorize a quote:

  1. Choose a quote from the menu.
  2. Tap a word once to hide it. To reveal it, tap it again.
  3. To hide words automatically, press the "Hide Words" button on the bottom nav.
  4. To show all of the hidden words, press the "Show All" button.
  5. To move to the next or previous quotes in the collection, press the forward and back arrows on the bottom nav.
  6. When you have memorized a quote, you can track your progress by tapping the check icon on either the quote selector or memorize screens.

Adding quotes

  1. To add a quote select the "Add Quote" option in the menu.
  2. Enter the quote, the author, the reference and choose the language of the quote. The language selection allows iMemorize to properly display quotes in languages such as Arabic, Chinese and Hebrew.
  3. Press "Save" and the quote will be added to your "Saved quotes" collection.
  4. Press "Save and Memorize now" and you can start to memorize the quote right away.

Editing saved quotes

  1. Select a quote in the "Saved Quotes" collection.
  2. Click the "Edit" (pencil) icon.
  3. Select either "Edit quote" or "Delete quote".

Sharing quotes

  1. To share a quote with a friend, first select a quote.
  2. Select "Email Quote" from the menu.
  3. Your email client will open and you can edit the text of the email as you wish.
  4. The email will include a link where your friend can use the online version of iMemorize to memorize the quote.

Additional options

  • To change the font size, select "Change font size" in the menu
  • If an underline appears below a reference, you can tap on it and your browser will open the quote in the original context on the internet.
